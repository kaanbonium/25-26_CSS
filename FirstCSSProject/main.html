Welcome to my cubing website, an official guide to increase your speed, record times, and knowledge of the cube. Before you actually dive in, I would recommend purchasing a speed cube! You can get good ones for only 10$, as you don't need the top of the line to get good times. I would also recommend buying lube to use on it, and a timer if you wish. The last thing you should do before using this guide is learning how to solve one, and I recommend CubeHeads 2025 tutorial. After that, basic CFOP, which consists of solving the cross without using a sunflower, learning f2l, 2-look OLL and PLL. It might sound out of reach, but once you learn this, you can start using the guide to its best ability. And lastly, remember to have fun!
