Understanding Algorithms

Algorithms are move sequences that perform specific cube functions — like orienting corners, flipping edges, or permuting the last layer.

Memorize algorithms in sets, such as F2L, OLL, and PLL. Build muscle memory by repeating each one slowly until it becomes automatic.
