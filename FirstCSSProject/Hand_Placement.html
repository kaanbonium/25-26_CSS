Efficient Hand Placement

Proper hand placement improves control, reduces strain, and increases turning speed. Keep thumbs on the front face and use index fingers for U-turns.

Avoid gripping too tightly — relaxed hands let you transition between moves fluidly and prevent fatigue during longer sessions.
