What Is Lookahead?

Lookahead is a core speedcubing skill. It means planning your next moves while executing your current ones, so you can solve continuously without pausing.

The best way to improve lookahead is to slow down your solves. Focus on tracking how each move affects your pieces. As you practice, your eyes and brain will naturally anticipate faster.
